Our Subsidiaries

NABARD has seven subsidiaries, established to leverage the institute’s expertise with its own specialists catering to specific sectors.

NABKISAN

Established 1997

Promotes agriculture and rural development through financial services to agri-businesses and FPOs.

NABSAMRUDDHI

Established 2004

Consulting company providing technical advisory and capacity building for rural development projects.

NABFINS

Established 2010

Microfinance institution supporting low-income households and SHGs with accessible credit.

NABFOUNDATION

Established 2020

Focused on social innovation and women empowerment through rural livelihood programs.

NABCONS

Established 2003

Consultancy company offering solutions in agriculture, rural development, and infrastructure sectors.

NABVENTURES

Established 2019

Venture capital arm investing in agri-tech, rural fintech, and allied sectors.

NABSanrakshan

Established 2016

Subsidiary managing credit guarantee funds and promoting sustainable development initiatives.
